    How the examination works

    From raw text to verdict, in four steps

    01

    Submission

    Your passage is received as plain text — no formatting or metadata is used.

    02

    Feature extraction

    Nine measurements are taken: sentence rhythm, vocabulary richness, word length, readability, punctuation density.

    03

    Random Forest vote

    200 decision trees, each trained on essays, news, and social text, cast an independent vote.

    04

    Verdict & confidence

    The majority vote becomes the verdict; the vote margin becomes the confidence score.

    What it actually measures

    Case notes

    Known limitations — read before trusting a verdict

    This model measures writing style, not authorship. Highly polished human writing — Wikipedia, professional journalism, careful non-native English — can score close to AI-generated text, because both are structurally clean. This is a documented, unsolved problem across commercial AI detectors, not unique to this tool. Treat results as one signal among several, never as proof.
